Learn the basics

Understand stocks before you predict them.

Quick beginner-friendly lessons that explain the numbers StockSensei uses on the prediction page.

1

What a stock price means

A stock price is what buyers and sellers agree one share is worth right now. It moves as people react to earnings, news, rates, and market sentiment.

2

Why moving averages matter

Moving averages smooth out daily noise. A 5-day average reacts faster, while a 10-day average gives a steadier view of short-term trend direction.

3

Volume shows participation

Volume is how many shares traded. Big moves with high volume can signal stronger interest than the same move on quiet trading.

4

Predictions are not guarantees

The model estimates a likely next direction from recent features. It can be useful for learning, but it should not be treated as financial advice.

Key Terms

OpenThe first price traded during the market day.
High / LowThe highest and lowest price reached during the selected period.
Daily ReturnThe percentage change from one close to the next close.
VolatilityHow much the price has been moving around recently.
ConfidenceHow strongly the model chose its predicted direction.
